Laguna State Polytechnic University Los Baños Campus

LSPU Los Baños Campus (LSPU LBC) is one of the key campuses of Laguna State Polytechnic University (LSPU), a premier state university in the Philippines known for its excellence in education, research, and community development.
Located in Los Baños, Laguna, this campus offers high-quality academic programs across various disciplines, particularly in agriculture, engineering, information technology, business, and education.

Strands that we offer

Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM),Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS),Information and Communications Technology (ICT), and Agri-Fishery Arts(AFA)

ICT

In Senior High School Information and Communications Technology is designed for students who are passionate about technology, computers, and digital communication. It provides hands-on training in software development, computer programming, networking, and multimedia technologies.

ABM

The ABM strand under the Academic Track in Senior High School is designed for students who are interested in business, entrepreneurship, finance, and management. It equips students with the fundamental skills needed to run a business, analyze financial statements, and lead organizations effectively.

HUMMS

The HUMSS strand under the Academic Track is designed for students who are passionate about writing, communication, social sciences, and public service. It focuses on understanding human behavior, cultures, politics, and societal issues, preparing students for careers that involve critical thinking, empathy, and leadership.

AFA

The Agricultural and Fishery Arts (AFA) strand under the Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) track is designed for students who are interested in farming, fisheries, agribusiness, and agricultural technology. This strand equips students with practical knowledge and hands-on skills in crop production, animal husbandry, aquaculture, and sustainable farming practices.

Our Facilitis

Laguna State Polytechnic University (LSPU) offers modern and well-equipped facilities across its four campuses to support academic excellence, research, and student activities.
These include libraries, computer and science laboratories, lecture halls, and specialized research centers, particularly in agriculture, fisheries, engineering, and business.
The university also provides sports complexes, gymnasiums, and open fields for physical activities, along with auditoriums and multipurpose halls for events and cultural programs.
Student services such as medical and dental clinics, dormitories, canteens, and career placement offices ensure a comfortable and supportive learning environment. Additionally,
LSPU enhances digital learning through Wi-Fi access, e-learning platforms, and student information systems, making education more accessible and efficient.

Library

The LSPU Library is a key academic resource center that supports learning and research across all campuses of Laguna State Polytechnic University. It offers a wide range of books, journals, online databases, e-books, and computer stations to assist students and faculty. The library provides a quiet and conducive environment for studying, research, and academic collaboration. It also hosts events and workshops to improve research and writing skills, playing a crucial role in the academic success of the university community.

Covered Courts

The LSPU Courts provide sports facilities for students, faculty, and staff at Laguna State Polytechnic University. These include basketball and volleyball courts, which are used for recreational play, physical education, and competitive sports events. The courts offer a space for promoting physical fitness, teamwork, and student engagement in sports activities.
